How To...

  • Make a Gift Online

    Our secure online donation portal is the fastest, most direct way to make a gift to Landmark College. 

    In addition to making a one-time gift, you also have the option of making monthly, quarterly or annual recurring contributions online. This is a great way to support Landmark College without having to receive a reminder letter or phone call. By making monthly or quarterly installments, you can spread your payments out over the year.  

  • Make a Gift By Check

    Please make your check payable to Landmark College and mail it to:

    Office of Institutional Advancement
    19 River Road South
    Putney, VT  05346

    If you wish to designate your gift for a specific purpose, please indicate so on your check.

  • Give to the Alumni Fund

    With your Alumni Fund gift, Landmark College can continue to provide students with a solid foundation for college and for life.

  • Make a Matching Gift

    Many employers offer matching gift programs. Check with your company's human resources office to see if they offer this benefit. If they do, just complete their matching gift form and mail it to Landmark College at:

    Office of Institutional Advancement
    19 River Road South
    Putney, VT  05346

  • Give Securities

    If you have owned long-term appreciated securities—such as stocks, bonds, mutual funds, or real estate—for more than a year, it may be beneficial to donate them instead of cash. 

    Learn more about donating securities

  • Give for Scholarships

    If you are interested in helping students for whom Landmark College might otherwise be out of reach, you may designate your annual fund gift to "scholarships." Your gift will then be matched through a Johnson Scholarship Foundation grant and will provide immediate assistance to students with the highest financial need.

    Alternately, you can create a named endowed scholarship which will provide a permanent, perpetual door to educational opportunity for students with financial need. To explore this option, please contact the Office of Institutional Advancement at 802-387-6734 or advancement@landmark.edu.

  • Donate Other Assets

    Your gift of tangible personal property—artwork, books, antiques, real estate and other similar items of value—may be eligible for donation. To discuss a possible donation of tangible assets, please contact Ellen Smith, Vice President for Institutional Advancement, at 802-387-6870 or elllensmith@landmark.edu.

  • Make a Federal Employees CFC Gift

    Federal employees can support Landmark College through the Combined Federal Campaign (CFC) program. When designating Landmark College as the recipient, use CFC designation code 47669 to indicate us as your charity of choice.
