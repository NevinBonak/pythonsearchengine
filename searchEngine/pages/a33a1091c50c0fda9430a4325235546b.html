Attend an Open House or Plan a Visit

prev next

Are you ready to “Fall Into a New Beginning?”

The best way to learn about a college is to experience it for yourself. Attend our Fall Open House on Saturday, November 18th and see first-hand why Landmark College is known as The College of Choice for Students Who Learn Differently. And, what better time to visit Vermont than in autumn?

Our Fall Open House includes interactive student classroom presentations, informative sessions, and demonstrations. You also get to tour our beautiful Vermont campus, meet with faculty, staff, and students, hear about Admissions, Financial Aid, Study Abroad, Athletics, etc. Finally, meet with an admissions counselor and apply for admission on-site.

Attendees who apply that day get entered into a raffle to win prizes such as a Virtual Reality headset, bookstore gift certificate and more.

Schedule a Campus Visit

If the Open House dates don't work for you, you can schedule an individual visit at your convenience, Monday through Friday, year-round. Individual visits include a meeting with one of our Admissions Directors and a tour of campus. Be sure to let us know if you would like to observe a class. Advance notice is required for class observations.
