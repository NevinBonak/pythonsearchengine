Our Core Values 

Teaching and Learning

  • We value students’ wholeness, humanity and resourcefulness.
  • We embrace students’ strengths.
  • We welcome students as partners in learning.

Academic Rigor

  • We hold high expectations, both for ourselves and for our students.
  • We challenge students to develop & employ critical thinking.
  • We support students in reaching their greatest academic potential.

Academic Support

  • We prepare students to meet high academic expectations.
  • We work closely with students, both in the classroom and individually.
  • We help students to develop a personal toolkit of skills and strategies for learning.
